Potatoes are great because you can eat them. You can mash them, fry them, or put them in a stew. Mashed potatoes are tasty with steak. Fried potatoes go good with cheeseburgers and sandwiches. When yo put potatoes in a stew you make that stew at least 10 times better. Potatoes are the best type of food because they are filling and taste good.
